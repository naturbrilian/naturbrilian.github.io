Deciding to retiring from my favourite game

Yes, I last wrote a post on this blog 8 months ago, which was about "Pensi atau Lanjut. Moreover, I rarely write on the blog not because I'm lazy or what, but I myself don't know what I want to write about, it feels very empty plus I myself am already stressed looking for work here and there, especially since WFH has no test info or what.

Yeah, actually the story above can be a problem where the more I get emotional with one of my favorite games, Princess Connect Re: Dive. This used to be one of my favorite games that I played when I was taking a break at work or if I was really tired. But increasingly here there are factors where I am getting bored to play this game for a long time, the first is that the game content is very boring for a long time, how not usually if there is a time-limited event I am very enthusiastic & why now I am not interested? because, I feel that the limited event is like being repeated (usually we use the term rerun. Secondly, the age factor, yes, please note that I myself am not like I used to be very enthusiastic about playing this game, now I'm not anymore & since there was an update about the system requirements for the device, I've just been really lazy.
