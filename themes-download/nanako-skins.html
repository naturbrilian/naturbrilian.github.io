Nanako Skins (V5)

The interface design is inspired by the concept of the Unhearted Music Player online player by Raafi G (unfortunately the Facebook post is no longer available) and Project FX_ by FrostX00001101. I initially created this because there was no suitable skin or appearance on the official site or deviantart. I deliberately created two versions. In the first version, you are free to edit & add background images.
